Recipes for Lasagna

Description

This is probably one of the best recipes in the world, like seriously just try it. Sometimes in my days I just wanted to try it out but it never worked out

This is the second paragraph of me trying to make my lasagna recipe page a hit across the galaxies

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Hot cooking oil
  • 2 litres of water
  • Noodles

Steps to prepare

  1. Wash your hands with clean water
  2. Start the fire
  3. Place the frying pan into the stove
  4. Pour in the oil
  5. Start cooking and it should be done in a matter of minutes
